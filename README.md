# 🚗 Road Accident Analysis Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

**🚦 Road Accident Analysis Dashboard**

An interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze road accident trends, identify high-risk conditions, and provide actionable insights into accident patterns based on time, road type, weather conditions, and road surface conditions.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Road Accident Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Excel-based reporting solution that transforms raw accident data into meaningful visual insights. It enables users to explore accident trends, monitor key performance indicators, and identify the major factors contributing to road accidents. This dashboard is intended for transportation authorities, road safety analysts, policymakers, and organizations seeking data-driven insights to improve road safety.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Microsoft Excel** – Main platform used for dashboard creation and data visualization.
- 📈 **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts** – Used for summarizing and visualizing accident data.
- 🎛️ **Slicers** – Implemented to provide interactive filtering across dashboard visuals.
- 🧹 **Data Cleaning & Preparation** – Performed using Excel formulas and formatting tools.
- 📐 **Data Analysis** – Excel functions and Pivot Table calculations used to generate KPIs and insights.
- 📁 **File Format** – `.xlsx` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

---

## 4. Data Source

**Source:** Road Accident Dataset

The dataset contains road accident records with information such as accident dates, road types, weather conditions, road surface conditions, districts, accident severity, and casualty details. The data was cleaned, transformed, and organized to support interactive reporting and trend analysis.

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### 📌 Business Problem

Road accidents result in significant loss of life, injuries, and economic costs every year. Analyzing accident data manually makes it difficult to identify trends, accident-prone conditions, and high-risk locations.

Key questions addressed include:

- Which months experience the highest number of accidents?
- Which road types are most accident-prone?
- How do weather conditions affect accident frequency?
- Which road surface conditions contribute to more accidents?
- Which districts report the highest accident counts?

---

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard aims to:

- Analyze road accident trends over time.
- Identify accident-prone road and weather conditions.
- Compare accident distribution across different categories.
- Support road safety planning through data-driven insights.
- Provide an interactive reporting experience using filters and dynamic visuals.

---

### 📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### 🚦 KPI Cards

Displays important business metrics including:

- Total Accidents
- Total Casualties
- Fatal Casualties
- Serious Casualties
- Slight Casualties

#### 📅 Monthly Accident Trend

Shows accident distribution across different months, helping identify seasonal patterns and peak accident periods.

#### 🛣️ Road Type Analysis

Compares accident frequency across different road types to identify high-risk road categories.

#### 🌦️ Weather Condition Analysis

Displays the impact of various weather conditions on accident occurrence.

#### 🛤️ Road Surface Condition Analysis

Illustrates accident distribution across different road surface conditions such as dry, wet, or icy roads.

#### 🎛️ Interactive Filters

Slicers allow users to filter the dashboard dynamically by various categories, making exploration easier and more interactive.

---

### 💡 Business Impact & Insights

- **Road Safety Planning:** Helps authorities identify accident hotspots and prioritize safety improvements.
- **Infrastructure Development:** Supports better planning for road maintenance and traffic management.
- **Policy Making:** Assists government agencies in creating targeted road safety initiatives.
- **Risk Assessment:** Enables analysts to understand accident patterns under different road and weather conditions.
- **Data-Driven Decision Making:** Provides an interactive dashboard for monitoring accident trends and improving transportation safety.
